#Bioinformatics Projects 

This repository contains bioinformatics and genomics projects completed as part of my academic and technical training in biomedical science and bioinformatics. 

#Projects
#### Soybean SNP Imputation Analysis ####
A genomics project focusde on genotype imputation using soybean chromosome 20 SNP data. The workflow involved VCF processing, SNP-density reduction, phasing, imputation, and accuracy comparison using bioinformatics tools.
*** Skills/tools used ***
VCF analysis, SNP filtering, Beagle, Minimac4, bcftools, vcftools, PLINK, Bash, Linux

#### Tomato Viral Pathogen Genomics ####
A viral genomics project investigating Tomato mosaic virus (ToMV) and Tomato chlorosis virus (ToCV). The project involved anaysing viral genome sequence data to compare sequence variation, conservation, and evolutionary relationships.
*** Skills/tools used ***
Sequence alignment, phylogenetic analysis, BLAST, MAFFT, seqkit, biological interpretation

#### RNA-seq Workflow Concepts ####
Coursework and learning activities related to RNA-seq quality control, normalisation, differential expression analysis, and biological interpretation of gene expression data.
*** Skills/tools used ***
RNA-seq QC, differential expression concepts, data interpretation, R
